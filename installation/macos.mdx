## Requirements

- macOS 13.0 Ventura or later
- Intel or Apple Silicon processor (the provided binary is universal)

## Download

<Card title="Download Chatterino7 for macOS" icon="apple" href="https://github.com/SevenTV/chatterino7/releases/latest">
  The universal `.dmg` or `.app` archive is on the GitHub releases page.
</Card>

The release is a **universal binary** — a single download runs natively on both Intel Macs and Apple Silicon (M-series) Macs. You do not need to choose between separate builds.

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the release">
    Go to the [releases page](https://github.com/SevenTV/chatterino7/releases/latest) and download the macOS archive (`.dmg` or `.zip`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the archive">
    Double-click the downloaded file. If it is a `.dmg`, drag Chatterino7 into your **Applications** folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Chatterino7">
    Open Chatterino7 from your Applications folder or Launchpad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bypass Gatekeeper if prompted">
    Because Chatterino7 is distributed outside the Mac App Store, macOS may display a warning that it cannot verify the developer. To open it:

    1. Go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security**.
    2. Scroll down to the Security section and click **Open Anyway** next to the Chatterino7 entry.
    3. Confirm by clicking **Open** in the dialog.

    You only need to do this once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your Twitch account">
    Go to **File → Settings → Accounts** and click **Add account** to authenticate with Twitch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a channel">
    Click the **+** button in the split area, or go to **View → Open channel** and enter a channel name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you are on macOS 12 Monterey or earlier, you must [build Chatterino7 from source](/building/macos). The minimum supported version for prebuilt releases is macOS 13.0 Ventura.
</Note>
