## What is Chatterino7?

Chatterino7 is a fork of [Chatterino2](https://github.com/Chatterino/chatterino2) — a desktop chat client for Twitch. It adds exclusive features for [7TV](https://7tv.app) subscribers and users that aren't available upstream, while staying up to date with the latest Chatterino2 improvements.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Chatterino7">
    Head to the [releases page](https://github.com/SevenTV/chatterino7/releases/latest) and download the installer for your platform. Windows users can also install via [Chocolatey](https://chocolatey.org/packages/chatterino7).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with Twitch">
    Open Chatterino7 and go to **File → Settings → Accounts** to add your Twitch account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a channel">
    Click the **+** button in the split area or go to **View → Open channel** and type a channel name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enjoy 7TV cosmetics">
    Your 7TV name paint, personal emotes, and animated avatar are automatically loaded if you have an active 7TV subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>
